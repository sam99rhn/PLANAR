How to Use the Graph Visualizer

Start Graph

Create a basic triangle to begin with

Add Vertex

Click two peripheral vertices to add a new vertex between them

Random Vertex

Automatically add a vertex at a random position

Toggle Display

Switch between showing vertex indices and color values

Go to Vertex

Enter a vertex index to center the view on that vertex

Zoom In/Out

Adjust the zoom level of the graph

Drag

Click and drag to pan around the graph

Mouse Wheel

Scroll to zoom in and out
